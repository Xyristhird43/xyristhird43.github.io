FAQ

Questions, Answered

How accurate are the revenue estimates?
Estimates are modeled from sales-rank velocity, review growth, and category-level conversion curves. They're directional tools for comparing opportunities — always validate with a test order or small launch before committing inventory.
Do I need an Amazon or TikTok Shop seller account to use NexLaunch?
No. All research tools work without a seller account. You'll only need seller accounts when you're ready to actually list and sell products.
What makes NexLaunch different from Helium 10 or Jungle Scout?
Those tools are Amazon-only. NexLaunch covers Amazon and TikTok Shop in one dashboard, so you can spot a product trending on TikTok and validate its Amazon economics (or vice versa) without switching tools.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel in one click from your account settings and keep access through the end of your billing period.
Does the AI Listing Builder work for TikTok Shop too?
Yes — it has separate modes. Amazon mode optimizes for search keywords and bullet-point scanning; TikTok mode writes shorter, hook-driven copy that matches how people buy in-feed.
